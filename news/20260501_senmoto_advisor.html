NexaScience Welcomes Sachio Semmoto as Advisor

The founder who rewired Japan’s telecom industry joins the team building AI’s execution layer

Tokyo, Japan — May 1, 2026

NexaScience, the AI infrastructure company on a mission to give AI real-world execution power, today announced that Sachio Semmoto has joined as a company advisor.

Semmoto isn’t just a legend on paper. He founded DDI (now KDDI), liberalized Japan’s telecom monopoly, then did it again with e-Access and e-mobile (now Y!mobile) to bring broadband to millions, and again with Renova to push renewable energy forward.

He holds a Ph.D. in Electronic Engineering and currently serves as Special Presidential Advisor and Distinguished Professor at Kyoto University, Presidential Advisor at Hokkaido University, and Visiting Professor at UC, ETH Zurich, and NYCU Taiwan.

Why This Matters

Generative AI efforts are accelerating globally, yet a significant gap still remains between advances in Foundation Models evolution and their real-world deployment.

With Sachio Semmoto joining us as an advisor—bringing decades of experience building infrastructure at national scale—we will strengthen the foundations that allow AI to connect and integrate with diverse systems and services, and accelerate the development of next-generation infrastructure built around AI.

From Sachio Semmoto

“I have spent my career at the intersection of technological breakthroughs and societal shifts. Telecom liberalization. Broadband. Clean energy. Each time, the pattern was the same: a technology with the power to reshape society, and a window to act before the window closed.

AI is that moment—multiplied.

What I see in NexaScience today carries the same raw energy and disruptive potential I felt during the dawn of the internet era. This team possesses a rare combination of elite research talent and the grit to deliver. I am joining NexaScience to ensure this innovation doesn’t just compete, but leads the global AI revolution.

I’ll bring everything I have.”

From Yoshitaka Ushiku, Founder & CEO

“At NexaScience, we believe that our mission is to build the global standard for AI execution.

To rewrite the rules of industry, you need more than just code; you need the vision to navigate massive structural shifts. Dr. Semmoto has done this three times over in telecom and energy. His joining our mission is a powerful validation that AI is the next great infrastructure frontier.”

About NexaScience

NexaScience is building the execution layer for AI—the infrastructure that turns breakthroughs into real-world impact. The team brings together leading AI researchers, world-class engineers, and operators focused on deploying AI at scale.
